About Gyanu Note

Your trusted companion for academic excellence

Our Story

Empowering Students Through Education

Gyanu Note was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality education accessible to every student in Nepal. We understand the challenges students face in finding reliable study materials, and we're here to bridge that gap.

Our platform provides comprehensive notes, textbooks, and study resources for students from Class 8 to 12, covering all major subjects. We believe that every student deserves access to quality educational content, regardless of their location or economic background.
